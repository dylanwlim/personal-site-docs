# Product Guide

## User experience

- Open the homepage and scan the profile, current work, prior experience, selected work, photo rail, resume, and contact entry.
- Use the Selected Work section to inspect problems addressed by Alpinix, TheFrenchMani, PharmaPath, Transcribble, and the portfolio construction tool.
- Open live public project links where available.
- Use the footer contact dialog, LinkedIn, GitHub, and resume links.

## Main workflows

- Portfolio review: scan selected work by problem, then open the live project or unavailable state.
- Resume review: open the public resume PDF from the footer.
- Contact: use the prepared email path or professional profile links.
- Content update: maintain public copy from the private content source and validate before publishing.

## Product principles

- Keep the interface direct, explicit, and honest about what the product can prove.
- Show unavailable or limited states intentionally instead of hiding important constraints.
- Keep public claims tied to behavior that exists in the product.
- Keep private source, operations, and customer information out of public documentation.
